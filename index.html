Welcome Message From Hon Don Chris

From the Desk of the Director General

Welcome to the official page of Honourable Chris Ugwu, dedicated to political support and engagement in Igboeze North and Udenu Federal Constituency. As the esteemed House of Representatives Member representing our constituency, Honourable Chris Ugwu has been a beacon of progress, dedication, and unwavering commitment to the betterment of our community. This platform serves as a hub for information, updates, and opportunities to collaborate in shaping a brighter future for our constituency. Join us in our journey to advance our region and empower our people through active political involvement and support. Together, we can achieve great strides and bring about positive change.

Dear fellow citizens of Igboeze North and Udenu Federal Constituency, We stand at a pivotal juncture in the trajectory of our community's development, and the moment has never been more significant for collective action and support. It is with great enthusiasm that we extend an earnest appeal to each one of you to join our support organization for Honourable Atu, the dedicated House of Representatives Member representing our constituency.
