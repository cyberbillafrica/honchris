*FROM POWER SECTOR EXPERT TO PEOPLE’S CHAMPION: THE DON CHRIS ADVANTAGE FOR 2027*

Igbo-Eze North and Udenu Federal Constituency, the future is calling — and it demands competence, capacity, and character.

Engr. Chris Ugwu (Don Chris) began his professional journey at the prestigious National Power Training Institute of Nigeria (NAPTIN), Nigeria’s foremost power sector capacity-building institution, where he was trained in power generation, transmission, and distribution systems.

In 2014, he joined the reputable Sahara Group as an intern engineer. Alongside other brilliant young engineers, he was deployed across the entire power sector value chain — gaining hands-on experience at Egbin Power Station, Transmission Company of Nigeria (Akangba), and Ikeja Electric — understudying the post-privatisation operational framework of Nigeria’s power sector.

His excellence earned him permanent placement at Ikeja Electric plc, where he rose to management level within the Business Units — demonstrating leadership, strategic thinking, and operational mastery.

Armed with deep technical expertise and strong managerial acumen, Engr. Chris Ugwu transitioned into entrepreneurship. He is the co-founder and Chief Executive Officer of Synergy Fields Limited, an engineering and consultancy firm specializing in construction and capacity building, with a strong focus on youth and women empowerment, headquartered in Abuja.

Today, he serves as the Chairman of Krischek Groups — comprising Krischek Engineering Services Limited, Krischek Haulage and Logistics Limited, Krischek Pharmacy and Stores, and Krischek Properties Limited — enterprises that have created jobs and empowered numerous young Nigerians.

Engr. Chris Ugwu is not just a politician — he is a seasoned technocrat, a job creator, a project manager, and a community-driven leader.

In 2027, Igbo-Eze North and Udenu Federal Constituency have a clear choice:
Competence over promises.
Capacity over noise.
Results over rhetoric.

This is the man to entrust with your mandate.
